Lunular

Lunular is an app that focus on nails customization that is integrated with augmented reality, allows users to create their own nail design and the test it out in real time using their phone camera.

The app supports nail salons by letting them upload information about their services and prices in the app, making it easier for users to find and choose the salon they want to go to.

The app also work as a support app for other social media by allowing users to record/save/post their custom nail design after customizing on the app.

The user is able to access many tools to edit the nails
