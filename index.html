INTRODUCTION

Our app focuses on nails customization integrated with augmented reality that allows users to create their own nail design and test it out in real time using their phone camera. The system also allows users to save their own designs, find salon stores near them, and browse through salon designs that they’ve made through the app.

User can easily switch between customize mode and AR mode to try on the designs they’ve made. Match your hand with the lines for our algorithm to pick up the precise location. Fetch previous designs you’ve saved and swipe the screen to quickly change to a new look!

Use your skills to customize the perfect nail design.

Bring your unique style to life with the many tools provided by our app: change skin tone, modify nail length, applying colours, gradients, jewels, glitter, stickers, and other accessories. User can also import sketches they’ve made into the workspace to work closer to their vision.
