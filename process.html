Who was our target audience

Our users want to easily customize nail designs with lots of accessory options and be able to browse different designs based on season, mood, trends, and events. They want to find salons with good and cheap service which is closer to thier locations. They also want to share, save, bookmark, and find some inspiration for the design they could get on their nails.
